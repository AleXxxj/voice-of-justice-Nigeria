# voice-of-justice-Nigeria

A public advocacy platform dedicated to exposing injustice, documenting human rights violations, and demanding accountability across Nigeria.

## About

Voice of justice Nigeria is led by Prince Abisoye Emmanuel. an entrapreneur, philanthropist, and public justice advocate committed to defending the rights of ordinary Nigerians.

## Cases

- **Patrick Peresoudei** - Death after alleged detention (Jan 2026)
- **Karowi Bala (Balamene Jona Binis)** - Missing since July 11, 2024
- **Bodmas Kemepadei** - Person of interest, declared wanted by police

## Legal Notice

All accused persons are presumed innocent until proven otherwise in a court of law. Information presented is based on testimonies, court filings, and public records.

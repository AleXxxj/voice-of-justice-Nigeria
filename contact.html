FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is my identity really protected?

Yes. If you check the anonymous option, we do not store your name, IP address, or any identifying information. All submissions are encrypted.

What happens after I submit?

Our legal team reviews submissions. If the information is relevant to active cases, it may be used in court filings or advocacy. You will only be contacted if you provided contact details.

Can I submit evidence anonymously?

Absolutely. Use the anonymous option and do not include identifying information in your files. We recommend removing metadata from photos before uploading.

What if I'm afraid to come forward?

We understand. Many witnesses are afraid. That's why we offer multiple anonymous channels including WhatsApp, Signal, and Telegram. Your safety is our priority.

Do you pay for information?

No. We do not pay for testimonies or evidence. We are a non-profit advocacy platform committed to justice, not a commercial enterprise.
